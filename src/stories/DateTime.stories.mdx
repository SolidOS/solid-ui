## shortDate

By default, converts e.g. '2020-02-19T19:35:28.557Z' to '19:35' if today is 19 Feb 2020, and to 'Feb 19' if not.

[API docs](https://solidos.github.io/solid-ui/docs/api/modules/_widgets_buttons_.html#shortdate)

<Canvas withSource="open">
  <Story name="shortDate (not today & without time)">
    {UI.widgets.shortDate("2020-01-01T15:43", true)}
  </Story>
</Canvas>

<Canvas withSource="open">
  <Story name="shortDate (with time, but not today)">
    {UI.widgets.shortDate("2020-01-01T15:43", false)}
  </Story>
</Canvas>

<Canvas withSource="open">
  <Story name="shortDate (today & without time)">
    {UI.widgets.shortDate(new Date().toISOString(), true)}
  </Story>
</Canvas>

<Canvas withSource="open">
  <Story name="shortDate (today & with time)">
    {UI.widgets.shortDate(new Date().toISOString(), false)}
  </Story>
</Canvas>
